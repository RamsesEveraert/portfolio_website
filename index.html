About Me (1998)

Hello! My name is Ramses Everaert, I'm a 25-year-old student studying Digital Arts and Entertainment with a focus on Game Development at Howest University in West Flanders. Since I was a child, I've been captivated by movies and their soundtracks, which sparked my lifelong love for music. I learned to play the piano to deepen my appreciation for music.

My fascination with storytelling and music extended to the world of games, where these elements combine to create immersive, interactive experiences. Over time, my passion for gaming grew, leading me to pursue a career in the gaming industry.

At school, I’ve been focused on learning Gameplay programming, AI Algorithms, and graphics programming. I’m really excited about the chance to apply what I’ve learned in an internship and help create games that players will enjoy and remember. I look forward to the opportunity to bring my creativity and technical skills to a dynamic development team.
